Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

Introduction

ApplyAI ("we", "us", or "our") is a Chrome extension that helps users generate personalized cover letters using Google’s Gemini AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: ApplyAI stores your resume, cover letter templates, API key, and personal details (such as name, email, etc.) locally in your browser.
  • No Server Storage: We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers.
  • API Usage: Your Gemini API key is used only to communicate directly with Google’s Gemini API for generating cover letters. We do not access or store your API key.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: ApplyAI does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate cover letters using the Gemini API.
  • To autofill job application forms with your personal details.
  • To store your data locally for your convenience.

Data Sharing

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with any third parties, except as required to interact with the Gemini API (per your request).
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell your data.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage API.
  • We do not transmit your data except to Google’s Gemini API, as required for the extension’s core functionality.

User Control

  • You can view, edit, or delete your stored data at any time via the extension’s settings.
  • You may uninstall the extension at any time to remove all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the extension and on our website.
